Google Shopping Setup & Ecommerce tracking

The holiday season is right around the corner and advertisers are no doubt already thinking about their holiday strategies. For many, Google Shopping Campaigns and Google Analytics eCommerce Tracking should probably be part of the equation.

Google Shopping Campaigns and Google Analytics eCommerce Tracking can be a powerful combination for advertisers. The sales performance as well as the ad performance of the products being sold on the advertiser’s website can be measured and analysed by using these two features. The new Lead Submission Form lets you bundle a request to implement both products for an advertiser in a single submission. Here is a screenshot of what you would need to do.

We wanted to use this space to emphasize what our team needs to successfully implement these products for your advertisers:

Google Shopping

  Ensure the website is Google Shopping compliant

  Ensure that there are no restricted products

  Ensure that website has a secure checkout(https:// in the URL for the Checkout page)

  Ensure that the existing Google Merchant Center account(if present) is not disapproved for policy non-compliance. If the advertiser doesn’t have a Google Merchant Center account we will walk the advertiser through the setup.

E-commerce Tracking

  Ensure that the advertiser has access to make changes on his/her website

  Explain that this will require for some amount of coding and will take some time

• About 20-30 minutes for commonly used platforms like (Magento, Shopify, Big Commerce, Volusion, etc.)

• About 40-50 minutes for other custom designed platforms.

• If the platform is unfamiliar to the Tag Team, we may need their web developer online.

  Let the advertiser know that a test purchase might be necessary to ensure code works correctly

  Please check if Google Analytics is installed before hand, if not then please request a full Google Analytics implementation on the Lead Form
Celebrating Success

“Compre de boa” is an online shoe store that began to use our services in Q3 2014. Their campaigns, at that time, had low performance and low return on investment, the cost of conversion was R$131.88, and their conversion rate was approximately 0.47%.

After consultation, the client agreed to make some changes in his account, which significantly improved the campaign’s performance. First, we created a new Google Shopping Campaign and later set up Dynamic Remarketing. All of these implementations were supported by the Tag Team.

The end result, an overwhelmed advertiser who increased spend for Black Friday, Christmas and Boxing Day.
