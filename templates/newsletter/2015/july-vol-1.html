Q2 Success Story: Volaris (MMS Two Mexico, SpLatam)
  Volaris is a very large SMB client in SpLATAM with more than $600,000 in quarterly spend.  Prior to Q2, they had been using Criteo for Dynamic Remarketing and were hesitant to transition to Google's product because of effort required for technical setup.  Augustin Serrate, the Account Strategist who manages Volaris, was able to convince them to migrate their entire remarketing budget to AdWords, in part because of the promise of implementation support.  The Tag Implementation Team quickly helped them implement in days what they expected to take months, creating a very happy customer.  
  Customer quote: The team that helped us was so nice and they knew a lot!  Thank you very much for the support!
