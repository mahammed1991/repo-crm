  What’s New:
Our team is constantly evolving to better meet your needs and the needs of our advertisers. This quarter we have launched implementation support for two new tasks:
DynamicX: Dynamic Remarketing has expanded to all verticals!  We are here to help your advertisers implement this exciting new functionality.  
Some tips to help us implement DynamicX for your advertisers:
  Ensure that the campaign is created with recommended daily budget and ad group bid
  Make sure that your campaign includes all auto-created lists
  Retrieve the code from the audiences tab and include it where indicated on the lead form - this helps us be crystal clear what you would like us to implement for your advertiser
  Let us know if your advertiser needs help with the feed in addition to tag - we can create the feed with 5 products or 10% of the total products (whichever is lesser)
  Be clear about which type of vertical the tag implementation is for
Website Call Conversions (“WCC”): This powerful new product allows advertisers to accurately measure and attribute conversions from calls. Our existing Call Conversions product allows advertisers track calls from their ad creatives; WCC extends this by attributing calls from the number on their website to their AdWords campaigns. Some tips to help us implement WCC for your advertisers:
Some tips to help us implement WCC for your advertisers
  Make sure the advertiser has created a ‘Calls from website’ conversion
  Make sure the telephone number on the website is in text format and not image format
  Generate the code and include it where indicated on the lead form - this helps us be crystal clear what you would like us to implement for your advertiser
  If necessary, educate the advertiser on how he/she can format the telephone number to suit the style and format of the website (this is something we typically do NOT do for them)

  What Your Peers are Saying  
 
Dikshi Mokkapati is a newbie rep for the UK/I region. She is also a frequent user of the Sales Implementation support provided by Regalix. Here is what she has to say about her experiences:
 
Q What trends do you see regarding the tag implementation support needs of the advertisers in the market segment you cover?
A Conversion tracking and remarketing are the most important task types for Newbie advertisers. Many have sales or enquiries as the main objectives for their AdWords campaign. Hence, conversion tracking is very important for them. We have also seen more advertisers want to use the Display network and setup Remarketing.
Q What are the most common advertiser objections that you hear while pitching Conversion Tracking? Any thoughts for how to overcome such roadblocks?
A The most common pushback I get is when the advertiser’s website was setup by a website designer. In these cases, the advertiser often is not sure how to go about installing code on the site. When this happens, I ask the advertiser if the designer has provided them the website login details. If they have the login details, I tell them that is all they need and our implementation specialists can help them with the rest of the code installation process.
​
Q Please share a few tips for how to best leverage sales implementation support.
A Live transfers can help immensely if the advertiser is already logged into the account, has the website open in front of him, and is willing to make changes.
It also helps to ask the advertiser to let their website designer (if there is one) that they want to implement the code and that the implementation specialist will be reaching out to them. This ensures that there is minimum pushback from the designer.

NOTE: Live transfers are currently only available in the US, UK/I, Germany & AUNZ. We are working on setting up lines for other regions very soon.
