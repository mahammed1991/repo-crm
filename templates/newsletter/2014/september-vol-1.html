FAQ

What is the best way to transfer leads for maximum success?

Live Transfers:

Post your consultation, if the advertiser has shown interest in implementing conversion tracking, transfer the call to our rep and fill out the form with basic details immediately. (Contact number, advertiser name, website, tag requested - CT, RM)

Is it OK to pitch GA Conversion Tracking if the advertiser doesn't have Google Analytics?

Absolutely! We will implement Google Analytics code at first and setup the goals and conversion tracking accordingly.

 

Note: Ideally, it will be great if the advertiser has already setup their GA account and have the tags ready.

It saves time for us to set it up during the call and retrieve the code.

What can cause a delay in implementation?

Beyond Agent’s Control:

Under Agent’s control

Missed appointments due to oversight or previous call running over (Fixes put in place to counter this issue)

If the advertiser not reachable

 

If the advertiser does not have the time to implement

 

If the advertiser does not have the login credentials (to the CMS or Adwords) required

 

If the advertiser shares details of webmaster for implementation (The implementation now depends on the availability of the webmaster)

 

In case of technically complexity: Needs more research / time / troubleshooting (more than 2 appointments required to close case)
