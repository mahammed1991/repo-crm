Google Shopping

Via GTM

What Regalix will do

  • We check if the product is eligible for Google Shopping.
  • Based on the CMS and advertiser's preference we suggest a method of uploading the feed. ( GoogleSheets, automatic uploads, api's for select platforms, platform specific feed extensions )
  • We create the datafeed and then upload it to the Merchant Center and ensure that the datafeed has no errors.
  • Once the feed is inserted, we setup a Shopping campaign on AdWords with the recommended bid and budget.
  • We also troubleshoot Merchant Center & Shopping Campaign related issues.
  • Post implementation queries are also resolved.
  • We offer assistance on basic campaign & feed optimization ( campaign analysis & analytical tools* ) with regard to Shopping only.
  • *
    Feed related optimizations, location targeting, dimensions, product sub- divisions, conversion time etc

What Regalix will NOT do

  • We do not resolve policy violation issues (Suspended Merchant Center accounts) best we can do is escalate to STO or SQO Teams and communicate their response to the Googler.
  • We do not advise advertisers on how to setup a shopping friendly website
  • We do not offer advice on campaign bid or budget.Websites that are " Revamping " are not supported until completely finished.
  • Products that are WIP are not supported either ( no image placeholders, coming soon in text etc ) as it may lead to immediate disapproval.
  • The Googler's recommended bid & Budget will be referred to.
  • We do not advice on whether the site meets Google's Shopping Policy Requirements.

Platform specific limitations

  • Google API integration for Custom Platforms, FTP uploads.
  • Rocket Web for Magento - Cron Job, feed schedule Shopify - Bulk Edits for certain attributes
  • Product/Product page optimizations for Google's Shopping requirements.

Task specific limitations

  • 100 % inventory upload for certain extensions/ plug in's ~ not always possible as products have to be configured to meet Google's feed specs manually/ one by one.
  • 40% ~ 50% manual upload not always possible but pitched anyway.

Basic Requirements

  • Admin Panel Access / FTP
  • Access to Adwords
  • Permission to make changes on website
  • Access to make Test Purchase/Dummy Order for Verification
  • GTM Access
  • Access to Adwords
  • Access to make Test Purchase/Dummy Order for Verification
  • Avg. TAT: 8.35 Days
  • Avg. Time on call : 90 Min

FAQs

Remarketing / RLSA:

Q
Does Regalix help with list creation?
A
No, unless the advertiser decided to create different lists basis different tags.

GA Remarketing:

Q
Does Regalix advise on the types of lists to choose? On custom segments?
A
No, we only implement the tag. No discussion / recommendations on the strategy of lists.

Dynamic Remarketing:

Q
What level of support does Regalix provide with regards to the feed?
A
Regalix PLA team sets up the feed if required before proceeding with the setup of dynamic remarketing. We set up around 20 products of the feed or the entire inventory depending on the shopping cart we are dealing with and the customer's buy-in.
Q
Do you help define variables, for example?
A
Yes, we do define the variables depending on the website framework we are dealing with. For custom built website, we set up customized dynamic remarketing codes to fetch all required custom parameters. Example, on www.tamarascottdesigns.com, we were able to identify and create all required custom parameters to enable dynamic remarketing.

All types of Google Analytics code.

Dynamic X Remarketing:

Q
Does Regalix support with upgrade to the new Dynamic X Remarketing tag, and is this considered to be a new implementation?
A
For Dynamic Remarketing implementation there is a need to retag/upgrade the current tag and it will result in a new campaign

From a technical perspective, the upgraded tag will be in the audience tab and the only thing to do is to select the vertical and then either:
- replace the full tag,
- add only the custom parameters.
Yes, it will be considered as a new implementation
