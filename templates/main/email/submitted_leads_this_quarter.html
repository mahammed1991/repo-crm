Best practices to follow: Help us ensure that all of your leads turn into wins by following these tips:
Warm up the advertiser by
  - Getting them excited about the product/feature by educating them on how it can help them meet their business goals
  -
 Confirm with the advertiser that they have admin access to their website (or make sure to capture their webmaster’s contact information)
  - Let them know that code implementations can take up to 90 minutes (depending on the complexity of their site)
Make sure you use the calendering function on the lead form to schedule a day/time for the implementation that the advertiser is comfortable with
 

Let the advertiser know that they should give you a heads up if they need to reschedule their appointment
Be sure to share with us any contact information for their admins/gatekeepers, should we have difficulty reaching the advertiser directly
 

Always use the lead form to submit both TAG & Shopping leads via the portal
